Mountain Panic

Mountain Panic is our retro title for the BBC Microcomputer, published by Retro Software.

You must guide Bill through various screens of treacherous caverns and come out with your health intact!

There are various items scattered throughout the game essential for your escape, the only thing you begin with is an initial clue; a scrap of paper from a colleague's notebook which mentions a mysterious sign broken into 4 pieces...

The game is finished and is available as a boxed floppy disk, just like the old days!
